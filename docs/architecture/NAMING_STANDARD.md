# PV1 Naming Standard

## AI Agents
Pattern:
pv1_<purpose>_agent

Examples:
- pv1_dispatch_agent
- pv1_backup_agent
- pv1_client_comms_agent

---

## AI Specialists
Pattern:
pv1_<purpose>_specialist

Examples:
- pv1_routing_specialist
- pv1_revenue_specialist

---

## Services
Pattern:
<domain>-service

Examples:
- approval-service
- audit-service
- policy-service

---

## Events
Pattern:
domain.action.state

Examples:
- job.created.pending
- crew.assigned.completed
- invoice.payment.received
